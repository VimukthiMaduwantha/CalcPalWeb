 Proposal Presentation

This proposal presents an innovative solution to assist primary school students suffering from Dyscalculia by offering a personalized and interactive approach. It focuses on identifying the specific type of Dyscalculia each student experiences and providing targeted activities to strengthen their weak areas. The goal is to make learning math more accessible and enjoyable, enhancing the students' confidence and mathematical skills through engaging methods tailored to their needs.
