Milestones

  • 1

    Topic Assesment

    • In this evaluation, the team presented the topic idea along with the reserach gap and importance to the panel. The proposed components were explained along with the rough methodology plan to which feedback and improvement ideas were given by the panel.

    • -> Outcome - Topic Accepted with Minor Changes.
    • -> Topic Evaluation Form document was submitted with the minor changes suggested.
    • -> No marks were allocated to this evaluation.
  • 2

    Proposal Presentation and Report Submission

    • In this evaluation, the team presented the proposed application with the proposed methodology, tool and technologies. The importance of the application along with the research objectives and gap were presented. A clear overview and the system and the 4 components of the team members were portrayed during this evaluation.

    • -> Marks Allocated - 12%
    • -> Proposal Reports (4 seperate reports) with a detailed overview of the proposed components was submitted.
  • 3

    Progress Presentation 1 and Submission

    • In this evaluation, the team presented the application after the 50% completion milestone. In terms of functionality the detection models for all 8 types of Dyscalculia, including the CNN and RNN models were completely implemented and tested, and the detection component of the application was functional. The remedial activities component for each subtype of Dyscalculia was in progress, during this assessment. The implementation of the general authenitication functionality was completed and demonstrated too.

    • -> Marks Allocated - 15%
    • -> Status document 1 (4 seperate reports on the 50% progress) was submitted.
  • 4

    Progress Presentation 2 and Submission

    • In this evaluation, the team presented the application after the 90% completion milestone. The detection and remedial activities components relevant to all 8 types of Dyscalculia were completely implemented. The models were deployed in a Flask application and all other backend microservices were deployed using Azure. All general functionality inlcuding the IQ service and user roles handling were completed implemented. The 10% remaining belonged to the progress dashboard implementation and user testing areas.

    • -> Marks Allocated - 18%
    • -> Status document 2 (4 seperate reports on the 90% progress) was submitted.
  • 5

    Reserach Paper Completion

    • A detailed reserach paper overlining the entire reserach was written including an background and introduction to Dyscalculia and it's subtypes, a comprehensive literature overview, the methodlogy of development and testing in detail and the results of the research. The paper was written in IEEE format and submitted to a conference.

    • -> Marks Allocated - 10%
    • -> Conference Submitted - The International Conference on Computer Engineering, Network and Intelligent Multimedia (CENIM)
    • -> Conference Metrics (H5-index) - 13
    • -> Paper Status - Accepted for publication
  • 6

    Final Evaluation

    • The final and most critical evaluation which consists of 2 main sections; the presentation with the demonstration and the viva. During the presentation a brief overview of the entire research is presented followed by the demonstrattion of the final industry read application.

    • -> Marks Allocated for final presentation and viva- 20%
    • -> Final Reports (4 seperate reports) with a detailed overview of the entire research component
    • -> Marks Allocated for final report- 19%
