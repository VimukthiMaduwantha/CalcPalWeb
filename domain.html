 Literature Survey

A comprehensive lierature review was done to gather information about previous research done in the field of Specific Learning Diasbilities specially Dyscalculia. A summary of the pros and cons of the researches when compared with each other and the CalcPal application is given below.

 Research Gap

Absence of a single solution that uniquely identifies for each variant of Dyscalcilia and provides level wise, interactive remedial activities for intervention accordingly. Additionally, there is also a lack of Speicifc Learning Disability related applications in Sinhala language with activities tailored to the Sri Lankan school syllabus.

 Research Objectives

Assist Primary school students suffering from Dyscalculia in a new and interactive way by,

  • 1
    Identifying the specific type of Dyscalculia students suffer from.
  • 2
    Providing relevant activities to improve their weak points.
  • 3
    Making learning math a more accessible and enjoyable experience for all students.
