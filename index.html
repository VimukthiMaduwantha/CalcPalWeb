About Us

About our Application

Our application is designed to screen and predict the risk of all types of Dyscalculia, a learning difficulty that affects mathematical abilities, and provide engaging remedial activities to help primary school students improve. Using advanced algorithms and assessments, the app identifies specific dyscalculia subtypes, offering personalized intervention plans through fun and interactive math activities. These activities are carefully crafted to make math more accessible and enjoyable, empowering students to build confidence and skills at their own pace. Our mission is to create an inclusive learning experience that helps all students thrive in mathematics, regardless of their challenges.
