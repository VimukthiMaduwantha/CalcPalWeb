 Research Paper

Our research paper focuses on the development of a mobile application designed to detect all eight subtypes of dyscalculia using various supervised learning binary classification models, along with CNN and RNN architectures. The paper highlights the literature comparisons, reserach gap and importance, the methodology in detail and the results and discussions.

 Final Reports

The four final reports(thesis) provide an in-depth analysis of the development and testing of our mobile application, component wise. Each report focuses on few key aspects, covering the comprehensive research on all eight subtypes of dyscalculia, detailing the design and implementation of supervised learning models, and exploring the development of remedial activities tailored to each subtype, and the the testing process. Together, these reports form a cohesive study that offers insights into both the technical and educational impact of the app.
